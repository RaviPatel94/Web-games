TIC TAC TOE

Welcome to Tic Tac Toe! This classic game of strategy
and skill pits two players against each other in a battle of
Xs and Os. The goal is simple: be the first to line up three
of your symbols horizontally, vertically, or diagonally on
a 3x3 grid. Whether you're a seasoned player or new to the
game, Tic Tac Toe promises fun and excitement for all.
Get ready to test your wits and enjoy the timeless challenge!
